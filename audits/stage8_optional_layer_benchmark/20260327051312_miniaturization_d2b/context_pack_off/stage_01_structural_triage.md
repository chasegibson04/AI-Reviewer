## abstract
We present a systems-level approach to optimize reaction conditions for the reductive amination of staurosporine (36) with eight aldehydes, eight ketones and 48 different reaction conditions. We employed XPhos G3 as catalyst in aqueous K3PO4/DMSO at room temperature on an ultra-high-throughput format using a 1,536-well plate to determine the best combination of base/cosolvent for each substrate.
